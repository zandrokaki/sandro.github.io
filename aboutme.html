Who Am I?

My name is Sandro and I studied Computer Science in Granada, Spain. I've been playing videogames since I was a child and I've always wondered how those worlds were created.

Currently, I live in Granada and I'm improving my programming skills on my own. I am a person who loves learning, programming and teamwork. I really enjoy presenting my opinions as well as listening to those of my colleagues.

Videogames are my passion and I've been doing training courses with Unreal Engine since last year. Nowadays I'm doing a couple of courses on C++ to specialize me in videogames programming.

For now, I'm looking forward to get a seat at the master's course about Videogame Programming next year in Madrid.
