Crypt Raider

Crypt Raider is a puzzle and exploration videogame
developed in Unreal Engine 5 with C++ and my first contact with Lumen Engine.

Help the bounty huntress to steal the valuable treasure hidden in the crypt. She will face traps and solve puzzles in order to complete her mission successfully.
