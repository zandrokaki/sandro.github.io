Toon Tanks

Toon Tanks is an action videogame
developed in Unreal Engine 5 with C++.

Control your tank and level it up destroying towers. Player will face normal towers which make normal damage and have less range, and sniper towers which make high damage with a really high range. When player levels up, tank health is increased.

On the image, there is an experience bar above that is empty and tank health below. When tank destroys towers, its experience is increased and its health is increased (Default value is 100).
